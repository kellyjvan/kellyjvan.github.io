Day Use

Non members are welcome to use the park for a daily fee. See the charts below for day use pricing.

The park is located on Highway 66 about 8 miles west of Keno, Oregon.

Park Rules

  • All guests must check in at the park host kiosk before engaging in park activities.
  • 15 MPH speed limit on all roads.
  • Fires are only allowed in fire rings.
  • Please, no pets at the park.
